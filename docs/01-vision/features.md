# Key Features

> **📘 Blueprint Document**: This describes the intended feature set and capabilities
> for BindingChaos. These represent our architectural vision and design goals. Some
> features are implemented, others are in development, and some are planned for future phases.

## Signal-to-Idea Pipeline
- **Signal Surfacing**: Raw observations and needs from community members
- **Signal Promotion**: Organic promotion of signals to structured ideas
- **Collaborative Refinement**: Ideas evolve through amendments, contributions, objections, forks, and merges
- **Traceable Lineage**: Complete history of how ideas emerged and evolved

## Emergent Clustering
- **Organic Group Formation**: Clusters emerge based on shared interests and engagement patterns
- **Dynamic Evolution**: Groups form, merge, and dissolve naturally over time
- **Collective Attention**: System reveals what the community is focusing on
- **Affinity Discovery**: Helps participants find others with similar interests

## Locality-Aware Coordination
- **Geographic Boundaries**: Support for local, regional, and global coordination
- **Contextual Relevance**: Information and connections relevant to specific localities
- **Cross-Locality Learning**: Knowledge sharing between different geographic areas
- **Privacy by Design**: Data scoped and protected by geographic boundaries

## Trust Without Identity
- **Reputation Systems**: Trust scoring based on contributions and interactions
- **Pseudonymous Networks**: Trust relationships without revealing real identities
- **Contribution Recognition**: Acknowledgment of valuable contributions
- **Community Building**: Organic formation of trusted collaborator networks
